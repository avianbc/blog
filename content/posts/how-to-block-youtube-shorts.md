## uBlock Origin with the [ublock-hide-yt-shorts filter list](https://github.com/gijsdev/ublock-hide-yt-shorts)

1. Install [uBlock Origin](https://ublockorigin.com/) for your browser (available for Chrome, Firefox, Edge, etc.).
2. Open the uBlock Origin dashboard by clicking the uBlock icon in your browser toolbar and selecting the gear icon.
3. Navigate to the "Filter lists" tab.
4. Scroll down to the "Custom" section and click on "Import..."
5. Paste the following URL into the text box: `https://raw.githubusercontent.com/gijsdev/ublock-hide-yt-shorts/master/list.txt`
6. Click "Apply changes" at the top of the dashboard.
